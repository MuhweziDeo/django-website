About Us

KIBz VISION LTD is a marketing and advertising agency that prides itself in high impact creative strategies that resonate with targeted audiences. These strategies translate into increased brand or service uptake leading to positive behavior change and impact. As a result, brands need to find ways to navigate this ‘new world’ and that is what we live to do. We are here to find ways to tell your story while building mutually beneficial relationships to create strong and healthy brands. Our Purpose We exist to help clients find and connect with their target audience through modified made solutions that bring about positive change.

Brief History

In 2011, KIBz VISION LTD was Co-founded in Uganda by a married couple of Mrs Irene Kayemba and Mr Abdul Aziizi Kayemba.

Our Vision

To be the most creative, effective advertising and marketing agency in Africa.

Our Mission

To create strategic partnership with clients through innovative brand strategies and effective project executions for optimal results.
